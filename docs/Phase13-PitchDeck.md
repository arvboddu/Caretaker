# Slide 6: Business Model

## Revenue Streams

### Primary: Transaction Fees
- **5% Platform Fee** on bookings
- Caretaker pays, not patient
- Example: $25/hr × 4hrs = $100 → $5 platform fee

### Secondary: Premium Services
- **Background Check Upgrade:** $15/verification
- **Priority Listing:** $9.99/month for caretakers
- **Insurance Add-on:** $25/month per booking

### Tertiary: Enterprise
- **Agency White-Label:** $999/month
- **Healthcare System Integration:** Custom pricing

### Unit Economics
```
Average Booking: $150 (6 hours × $25/hr)
Platform Fee: $7.50 (5%)
Gross Margin: 85%+ (minimal operational cost)

Target CAC: $25
Target LTV: $450 (6 bookings/customer)
LTV:CAC Ratio: 18:1
```
