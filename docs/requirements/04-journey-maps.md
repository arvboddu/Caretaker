## Key Pain Points Summary

### Patient Pain Points
1. **Verification wait time** - Delays getting started
2. **Complex profile setup** - Too many fields, feels invasive
3. **Uncertainty about caretakers** - Can't fully verify quality
4. **Availability gaps** - Limited slots for desired times
5. **Rating friction** - Forgets or finds it tedious

### Caretaker Pain Points
1. **Long verification process** - Days to get approved
2. **Uncertain pricing** - Don't know competitive rates
3. **Limited patient info** - Can't fully assess compatibility
4. **Cancellation impact** - Lost income from no-shows
5. **Negative review handling** - No response mechanism

## Opportunity Areas

| Pain Point | Solution Opportunity |
|------------|---------------------|
| Long verification | Video verification option |
| Complex forms | Progressive disclosure, auto-save |
| Uncertainty about quality | Video introductions, trial visits |
| Rating friction | In-app prompt at service completion |
| Limited patient info | Rich patient profiles (with consent) |
| Cancellation losses | Cancellation policy with clear terms |
